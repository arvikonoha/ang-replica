What is ANG?

ANG is a Training Service Company driven by the vision and passion of Arcot Nataraj Gayathri (Founder and Chief Mentor).

ANG positions itself as a guiding force behind the success of every student who chooses our service by providing them with the assistance and guidance that they need to take the next BIG leap in their career.

We engage in the business of providing technical training and skill-based certification courses for freshers & experienced in different fields by various modes.

This includes...

  • Training and placement for students
  • Building confidence
  • Enabling self-analysis
  • Identifying potential self establishments

Our mission

Infuse passion into individuals and help them perform to the fullest of their potential. ANG's aim is to reduce employability gap and lack of skill set by providing training and workshops to build skills in the preferred area. We help individuals perform to their fullest, enhancing and empowering each one with what they are capable of.
